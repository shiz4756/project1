Stunning landscape in Guilin

Introduction

    Guilin in southern China is known for its thousands of limestone karst hills that provide a breathtaking landscape. One of the top tourism destinations in China, Guilin even has a famous landscape featured on the 20-renminbi bill in Chinese currency.A popular Chinese saying is "Guilin's scenery is best among all under heaven."

    In addition to majestic limestone hills and mountains, Guilin also features magnificent rivers, lakes and caves. To visit Guilin's unique natural landscape, each wonder takes several weeks.

    Guilin gave inspiration to a galaxy far, far away when its scenery was used for Star Wars: Episode III, Revenge of the Sith. Though no actual filming took place there, Lucas Films used plate photography of Guilin's limestone hills for backdrops to represent the planet of Kashyyyk.Kashyyyk was the planet of the Wookiees where Chewbacca was born.

    As a small city with only 4 million people, Guilin has the highest number of high-speed rail stations in all other cities in China. Guilin is 325 miles away from Hong Kong, but it only takes more than 3 hours by high-speed rail to reach a maximum speed of 180 mph.

    Guilin is famous for its unique hot sauce. This sauce is made from fresh peppers, garlic and soy. Guilin is also famous for rice noodles, which can be fried or eaten in soup.

Dragon Backone's Rice Terraces

    Dragon Backbone's Rice Terraces is the most amazing terraced field in China! The construction of the terraced field began in the Yuan Dynasty (1271-1368) and continued until the beginning of the Qing Dynasty (1644-1911) until the construction was completed. The Dragon Backbone's Rice Terrace is the crystallization of the wisdom of the Zhuang people and the hard work of the Zhuang people.

    The Dragon's Backbone Rice Terraces is a beautiful picture of nature. The linked terraces vary from season to season. In the spring, water is irrigated into the fields, and the terraces look like huge chains or ribbons hanging from the hillside. When summer came, the turquoise waves rushed down from the sky along the mountainside. The theme of autumn is harvesting, and the hillside is decorated with ripe millet gold. In winter, the entire mountain will be covered with snow, just like a dragon playing in water.

    In addition to the charming scenery, Longji is also a place where you can experience the culture of Chinese ethnic minorities. The Zhuang and Yao people live here, although mainly Zhuang. Women wore unique and colorful costumes, singing and dancing. Guests can also enjoy the original Zhuang life and culture with them, and even enjoy Longji tea and Longji wine with local families.

Du Xiufeng

    Its 66-meter peak towers in the center of Guilin, a lonely beautiful mountain peak) is a Guilin scenic spot that cannot be ignored. As the main sightseeing spot on the city's skyline since ancient times, the lonely and beautiful mountain peak is the creation of nature, and its name is well deserved. Standing alone in the middle of the streets, shopping malls and shops of Guilin, the top of the mountain is a blessing for nature. Standing in the middle of the famous Jing Jiang Wang Cheng, today it is known as the local symbol of Guangxi Normal University.

    Lonely Peak stands in the middle of Guilin's urban area. For thousands of years, it has been the name card of the people living next to it. Compared with the "pillar under southern heaven", this majestic mountain is a unique wonder in the world. Travelers who want to learn more about this peak and its history will climb to the top, albeit steep and difficult. In return, you can be like a gift from nature: magnificent views, a relaxing feeling, and a deep breath in the most beautiful scenery in heaven. When you plan to visit this mountain peak, the stay in Wang Cheng is also a great highlight, because tourists will know more about the history of Guilin city itself. The lonely beautiful mountain peak is a must-see scenic spot in Guilin, don't miss the opportunity to take a look there.

Guilin Elephant Trunk Hill

    Guilin Elephant Trunk Hill, also known as Elephant Mountain, is located at the conjunction of the Li River and the peach blossom river. It is the symbol of Guilin. The whole mountain looks like a huge elephant stretching its nose to drink the water of the Li River. It is a masterpiece of karst landforms, composed of pure limestone deposited 360 million years ago on the sea floor.

    Between the "trunk" and "body" is a huge circular cave, which is like a full moon on the water, hence the name "Moon-over-Water Cave". The water is rippling, the moonlight is shining, and the scenery is particularly charming. Throughout the ages, this cave has intoxicated countless poets. Many lines can be found to give this cave a high honor.

    On the top of the mountain stands a pagoda-Puxian Pagoda. It was built in the Ming Dynasty and has a long history. At the foot of the mountain is Yunfeng Temple. Inside it is the exhibition of The Revolution of the Taiping Heavenly Kindom in Guilin.
