Du Jiangyan

History

    The oldest irrigation system in the world is in China. It is called Dujiangyan and is the only ancient non-dam irrigation system that has survived. Dujiangyan is a miracle of science and engineering in China, built more than 2200 years ago. The system is still used to irrigate 66,700 hectares of farmland, drain water, and provide water for more than 50 cities in Sichuan Province.

    Dujiangyan is the wisdom of Li Bing, a local official in Sichuan Province, more than 2000 years ago. It appeared to respond to frequent floods of the Minjiang River, a tributary of the Yangtze River. Li Bing and his son found that the river flooding was caused by melted water from the mountains breaking down the bank of the slowly flowing river below. Building a dam was impossible because Li Bing was told to open waterways for warships. Therefore, Li Bing’s solution was to construct an artificial dike to transfer part of the water to another area, and then dig a ditch in Yulai Mountain to transport the excess water to the arid Chengdu Plain.

Scientific Value

    The famous dam-free water diversion project, Dujiangyan, has lasted for more than 2260 years. Its sustainable use of water treatment has high scientific value. The main project of Dujiangyan, the fish mouth, the flying sand weir and the treasure bottle mouth, is well-conceived. Since its creation, it has been repaired many times, but each repair has inherited this form of engineering hub layout, which shows that the planning more than 2000 years ago is simply Called "perfect". Yuzui, historically known as Dujiangyuzui, also known as water-dividing fishzui, has turned the water of the Minjiang River into two parts: Neijiang and Waijiang: Waijiang is the mainstream of the Minjiang River and flows to the Yangtze River; Neijiang water enters the Chengdu Plain through the Aquarium. "Deep beach, low weir", the "low" of Feisha weir was carefully observed and calculated by the ancients. Excess water can overflow from the top of the weir into the outer river to ensure the safety of the irrigation area. A section of the Inner River in front of the treasure bottle mouth was cut into a curve to achieve "front-side water intake and side-side sand drainage", which not only achieved a sufficient amount of water, but also avoided channel siltation. It is precisely because of Dujiangyan, which is in line with scientific principles, that the country of Tianfu, which "floods and dries from people, and does not know famine", was created.

Attractions

1. The two Kings Temple

    The two Kings Temple and Dujiangyan Water Conservancy Project, Lidui Park and Yulei Mountain together form the Dujiangyan Scenic Area. It is a temple dedicated to the excavator of Dujiangyan in China, the prince of Qinshu County, Li Bing and his son Erlang. Behind the mountains and the water, Gubai Sensen, with many cultural relics, is known as the “Yulei Xiandu”. The two Kings Temple was first built in the Northern and Southern Dynasties, and the existing building was rebuilt in the Qing Dynasty. The original name was Chongde Temple. After the Song Dynasty, Li Bing and his sons were named kings. After Qing Dynasty, they changed to the current name. The stone wall in the temple is embedded with Li Bing and the posterity’s motto about water control: deep beach, Low Zuo Weir, etc., which is called the three-character script of water control. There are statues of Li Bing and Erlang in the temple. The teahouse on the east side of the main hall is a hanging footbridge near the cliff, where visitors can relax and drink tea. On the right side of the apse are inscriptions by painters Zhang Daqian and Xu Beihong. The garden is full of various precious flowers and trees, towering ancient trees and shaded by trees, making it a tourist attraction in Sichuan.

2. Peaceful bridge

    "Peaceful bridge" is one of the famous five ancient bridge in our country, across both inside and outside the simultaneous, total length of 282 meters, the three main body is connected the dujiangyan irrigation project (fish mouth, fly sand dam, BaoPingKou), two Kings temple buildings and southern ancient silk road branch - matsushige important hub of mannequins Mang (tao). This bridge has a long history and was called "zhupu bridge" before the song dynasty. In the first year of chunhua in the song dynasty, after the reconstruction of the rope bridge by liang chu and yongkang army in Dali, the bridge was named after liang chu. The end of the Ming dynasty was destroyed by war. In the 8th year of jiaqing (AD 1803), he xiande, a scholar from guxian county, proposed to build a bamboo rope bridge with wooden boards as the bridge deck. The folk to commemorate the he couple, also known as the "husband and wife bridge."
